Trust

Child Safety

DreamScapes is designed for parent-led story creation. Stories should be warm, age-aware, non-frightening, and suitable for children.

  • Parents can add topics to avoid before generating.
  • Bedtime calm mode softens exciting moments.
  • Generated stories should end positively and avoid unsafe instructions.
  • Production AI will need server-side safety checks before launch.
