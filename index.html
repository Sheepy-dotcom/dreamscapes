Trust

Child Safety

DreamScapes is designed for parent-led story creation. Stories should be warm, age-aware, non-frightening, and suitable for children.

  • Parents can add topics to avoid before generating.
  • Bedtime calm mode softens exciting moments.
  • Generated stories should end positively and avoid unsafe instructions.
  • Production AI will need server-side safety checks before launch.

Trust

Privacy

This prototype stores saved stories and plan state on this device only. A production version should use secure accounts, encrypted transport, and clear parent consent.

  • Do not collect more child information than needed.
  • Explain how story prompts and audio are processed.
  • Let parents delete stories and account data.
  • Add a formal privacy policy before accepting payments.
