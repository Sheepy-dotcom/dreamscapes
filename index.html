Legal & Support

DreamScapes Policies

DreamScapes is a parent-led children's story app. This page brings the main terms, privacy, subscription, safety, deletion, and contact information together in one place.

Terms of Use

  • DreamScapes is designed for parent-led story creation, not unsupervised child use.
  • Stories are generated from the details you provide, such as name, mood, duration, and story idea.
  • AI-generated text and audio can vary, so parents should check every story for suitability.
  • Do not enter private, sensitive, or unnecessary personal information about a child.

Privacy

  • DreamScapes uses account details to sign you in, save stories, and manage your plan and usage.
  • Story prompts, generated stories, saved stories, and audio may be processed by trusted services used to run the app.
  • We aim to collect only what is needed to create and save stories for parents and carers.
  • You can ask for account or story data deletion by contacting support.

Subscriptions

  • Free, Premier, and DreamScapes Plus plans may include different story, saving, duration, and audio limits.
  • Mobile app subscriptions are planned to be handled through the Apple App Store and Google Play.
  • Renewals, cancellations, refunds, and payment management will follow the app store used to purchase the subscription.
  • Audio narration, saved stories, and other features may depend on account status, usage limits, and service availability.
  • AI audio may occasionally fail, sound uneven, or generate incorrectly. If this happens, try again and contact support so we can review the issue and help with credits where appropriate.

Child Safety

  • Parents can add topics to avoid before generating a story.
  • Bedtime and calm options are intended to soften exciting moments.
  • DreamScapes aims for warm, child-friendly stories with positive endings.
  • The app may refuse, shorten, or adjust requests that are unsafe or unsuitable for children.

Account and Data Deletion

  • Parents can delete saved stories from the library where deletion is available.
  • To request full account deletion, email support from the address linked to your DreamScapes account.
  • We may need to retain limited records where required for security, billing, or legal reasons.

Contact

support@dreamscapes.cloud

  • Contact support for account access, saved stories, missing audio, safety concerns, subscription questions, or deletion requests.
  • Include the email address used for your account and a short description of the issue.
  • This is a product draft and should be professionally reviewed before full commercial launch.
